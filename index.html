About play:

It is a free, non-imposed activity, that is, the individual does it on his own, free and willing, so that he can stop it of his own free will. It is an activity that does not aim for an end or result. It is also a directed or undirected activity in which the body’s motor and mental energy is exploited. It is characterized by speed, lightness, and difference. The difference between play and seriousness lies in the individual’s attitude and not in the type of activity



Harmful effects of games in general:

  1. Feeling anxious and stressed if he is unable to play

  2. Players may use inappropriate language
  3. Other forms of harm can occur when using the Internet for gaming such as harassment
  4. Many popular games promote violence and this can affect mindsets and behaviours, promoting aggressive thoughts, feelings and behaviours.

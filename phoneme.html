Manual games:

Practical learning is learning while trying something, and these activities include using the hands, such as teaching children mathematical concepts by counting cubes and sorting things. Manual games have advantages and benefits, including: They help your children develop their skills faster, as they are more fun for children; They get bored with pen, paper, and flashcards.

Chess is a strategic board game for two players. The game aims to move different types of pieces that have a set of possible moves on a square board. All moves aim to capture the main piece, which is called the king.

Billiards, also known as stick sports, is a variety of games played with a stick (called a pool cue) to hit and move a set of balls on a cloth-covered table bordered by flexible bumpers known as cushions. It is very difficult to determine who invented the game of billiards, as the history of the game is very complex.

Darts game: The player aims at a disk divided into five rings of different colors, but in a specific order from the outside to the inside (white - black - blue - red - yellow). The points range from 1 to 10 depending on the color and area of ​​the ring. If he throws the dart into the white ring, the The shooter has scored a goal, but if he shoots in the yellow ring, he will have achieved 10 goals

Popular games:

They are simple games that children use, generation after generation, without prior organization. They are an integral part of the cultural and popular heritage, as they are considered a means of entertainment and amusement to spend free time, and children resort to them to alleviate the harshness and difficulty of life.

Rope jumping game: It is played with a rope that is held at both ends. In the middle of the rope, the playerstands and then begins to jump, and the rope is wrapped around him as he jumps.

Rollerball game: where the players stand behind the drawn line and each player, in order, throws his wheel towards the circle. After the players finish throwing their wheel from behind the drawn line, the first player returns to throw his wheel to hit the wheel in the circle.
